# Problem Statement

The Museums of Image and Sound associated with ABPMIS preserve important audiovisual collections distributed throughout Brazil. However, this information is decentralized and organized using different standards, making it difficult to obtain a consolidated view of the national heritage represented by the association.

The lack of standardized indicators limits institutional communication, strategic planning, collaboration among museums, and the production of reliable information for researchers, government agencies, and funding organizations.

---

# Business Objectives

- Consolidate collection data from associated museums.
- Standardize the collection of institutional information.
- Provide strategic indicators for decision-making.
- Increase the visibility of the audiovisual heritage preserved by the associated museums.
- Promote collaboration among museums through shared information.

---

# Initial Functional Requirements

## FR-001

The platform shall allow museums to provide complete, partial, or estimated collection data.

## FR-002

Every quantitative information shall include a confidence level.

Possible values:

- Complete Inventory
- Partial Inventory
- Estimated Value
- Not Available
